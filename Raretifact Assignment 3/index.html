The Golden Mask

King Tutankhamun's burial mask contains 22 pounds of gold and is valued at over $2 million, making it one of the most valuable artifacts ever discovered.

Discovered 1922

Dead Sea Scrolls

These ancient manuscripts, dating from 300 BCE to 100 CE, are among the oldest surviving copies of biblical texts, preserved in clay jars for over 2,000 years.

Discovered 1947

Rosetta Stone

This granodiorite stele became the key to deciphering Egyptian hieroglyphs, written in three scripts: Ancient Greek, Demotic, and Hieroglyphic.

Discovered 1799
